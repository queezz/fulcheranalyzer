fit_vibro_ishi()[source]#

Ishihara-s vibro fit Fit vibrational population, ignoring rotational distribuiton to get Tvib. See formula (4.5) from Ishihara-s Master thesis, page 36. There R-coefficient is not rotationally resolved and hence does not include the branching ratio.

\(n_{dv'} \sum{A^{dv'}_{av''}} \propto \sum_{v=0}^{vmax} R_{Xv}^{dv'} \exp{\left( -\frac{E_{vib}^{X}(v)}{kT_{vib}^{X}} \right)}\)
